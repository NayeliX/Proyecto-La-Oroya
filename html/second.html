The Ebony Forest

Near Havarguard, Winter Territory

Forest of Nightmares

The Ebony Forest is called such because of the ebony colored trees that call this land home. It's thick canopy prevents any light from reaching the forest floor, making it the darkest forest in the land. Because of this, many dark creatures have found solice in these woods. Their dark magic has seeped into the roots of the trees, bringing life and sentience to the land itself. Anyone unfortunate enough to end up lost in these woods are guarenteed to never be seen again.
